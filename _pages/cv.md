# Short biography
Kristóf Reizinger is a PhD student at the Vienna Graduate School of Finance, a joint program of the Vienna University of Economics and Business, the University of Vienna, and the Institute for Advanced Studies. His research focuses on financial intermediation, fintech and innovation, payments, empirical corporate finance, and financial stability.
